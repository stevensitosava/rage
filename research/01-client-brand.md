## Brand Rationale

### Why Terracotta Coral?
Terracotta evokes Mediterranean warmth — the sunlit walls of an Italian piazza, the terracotta pots overflowing with herbs. It's distinctive in a category dominated by pastels and pinks, and positions Raffy Gelato as *artisanal* rather than *playful*. It photographs beautifully against cream backgrounds.

### Why Cormorant Garamond?
An elegant serif with Italian character — it signals craftsmanship, heritage, and premium quality without being stuffy. Used for headlines and display text only. Body copy stays clean and readable with Nunito.

### Tone of Voice
- Warm but not cutesy
- Confident but not arrogant
- Storytelling-driven ("Every scoop has a story")
- Personal — as if Raffy himself is talking to you

### Core Value Proposition
**"Handcrafted gelato made the Italian way — slow, real, unforgettable."**
